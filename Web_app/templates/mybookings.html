Photographic Art
Leonardo da Vinci

To pinpoint the beginning date of photographic art not possible. For this reason, we can define the beginning of the art of photography by describing the first inventions on optical inventions and image stabilization. In the 1500s,Leonardo da Vinci revealed the optical rules of all cameras, from the simplest to the most advanced, by saying, "If a lens is inserted into a hole to be opened on the wall of a dark room, the image of the outside landscape will be reflected on the opposite wall".

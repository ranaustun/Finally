STUDIO

Studio Reservation Application brings together several operators who own studios with varied concepts, with their clients on special occasions, who are in partnership, who want professional photography, and for a variety of other reasons. The application belongs to a multi-vendor booking marketplace. Through the application, customers choose a studio between various offerings that is suitable for their concepts, compare and contrast the pricing of different suppliers, then pick a specific date and time, after all, pay for it. This type of technology is essential for the organization since it is constantly available and allows clients to book at any time and any place. It satisfies the needs of organizations' clients since it is available online, is simple to use, and can be accessed from any device.
